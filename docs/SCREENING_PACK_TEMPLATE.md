## 7. Recommended Next Steps

For each Tier 1 target before any field expenditure:

1. **Land status check** — BLM MLRS / state mineral records. Confirm open and uncontested.
2. **Satellite review** — Google Earth / Sentinel imagery for outcrop, access, alteration color.
3. **Literature search** — State geological survey reports, historical prospect records.
4. **Geochemical gap assessment** — Is this target in a NURE sampling void? If yes, surface
   sampling is the most cost-effective next step.
5. **High-resolution geophysics** — If any of the above are positive, acquisition of drone
   gravity or ground magnetic data will localize the anomaly to drillable accuracy.

## 9. Limitations & Disclaimer

- Outputs are prospectivity indicators, not drill targets.
- Continental gravity resolution (~2km) precludes detection of small or structurally-controlled
  deposits in geologically complex terranes.
- Gravity inversion is non-unique. Depth and density estimates carry inherent ambiguity.
- Land status, existing claim records, and environmental constraints are the buyer's responsibility
  to verify before any field activity.
- This pack does not constitute geological, legal, or investment advice.
